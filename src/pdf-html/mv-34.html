Electronic Title & Registration (ETR)

Change of Address Affidavit

Purpose of this Affidavit: This affidavit is to be used to record a change of address for an ETR transaction. Please note: This address change affidavit must be submitted even if the vehicle purchase is cancelled.

Completing this Affidavit:Complete sections A, B and C (if applicable). Certify by signature and notarization that your address is correctly printed or typed.

How to submit this Affidavit:An electronic image of this completed change of address affidavit must be submitted electronically to the Department of Revenue within fifteen (15) days from the date of the change of address transaction.
